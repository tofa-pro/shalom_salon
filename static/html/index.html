About Shalom Braids

Where tradition meets innovation in hair artistry

Crafting Beauty Since 2018

At Shalom Braids, we believe that hair is more than just a styleâ€”it's a statement, a form of self-expression, and a work of art. Founded in 2018, our studio has become a sanctuary for those seeking exceptional braiding craftsmanship in the heart of Stone Mountain.

Our mission is simple: to provide every client with a transformative experience that combines traditional braiding techniques with contemporary styles. We specialize in a wide range of braiding services, from classic box braids and cornrows to intricate specialty designs and protective styles.

What sets us apart is our attention to detail, commitment to quality, and dedication to client comfort. We use only the highest quality hair extensions and products, ensuring your style not only looks amazing but lasts longer too.
